# Taqdimah - Dawah & Islah Ideas
## Only What Is Good and Beneficial for the Ummah

**Ummah welfare and coordination infrastructure for societal islah** - we only develop and surface what strengthens the Ummah.

**Purpose of this document:**  
Capture ideas **only for what is good and beneficial for the Ummah** - dawah (calling to Allah and good), Islamic education, tarbiyah, community strengthening, moral reform, family, charity, and essential services that help Muslims live with dignity and focus on their deen.

Nothing is included that weakens iman, promotes unnecessary dunya, or is not clearly a net positive for the Ummah.

Taqdimah is a public-benefit system for societal islah. We only put forward what is truly good.

---

## Core Principles for All Dawah/Islah Features

**Overarching rule:** We only develop and surface things that are clearly good and beneficial for the Ummah. 

If there is any doubt whether it strengthens iman, knowledge, family, community, or dignified halal living - it does not belong in Taqdimah.

We will actively reject or remove anything that is not a net positive.

1. **Verification first** - Especially for scholars, da'ees, content creators, and institutions. L4 Islamic verification is non-negotiable for religious guidance.
2. **Adab & humility** - Language, UI, and flows must reflect Islamic manners. No arrogance, no fitnah.
3. **Facilitation, not authority** - Taqdimah connects. It does not issue fatwas or replace qualified local scholars.
4. **Accessibility** - Bengali + English. Mobile-first. Free or very low barrier for seekers of knowledge.
5. **Transparency** - Every contributor's verification status is visible.
6. **Barakah focus** - Prioritize features that increase iman, good deeds, and community bonds.
7. **Sustainability alignment** - Dawah tools are preferably supported by waqf, institutions, or voluntary means. We avoid anything that commercializes pure dawah.

## 1. Strengthened Scholar & Da'ee Infrastructure

- **Verified Scholar/ Da'ee Profiles** (beyond current Quran teachers)
  - Upload of ijazah, sanad, madrasa affiliation, areas of expertise.
  - Topics: Aqeedah, Fiqh, Family, Dawah methodology, Youth guidance, etc.
  - Language and format filters (in-person, Zoom, sisters-only, etc.).
  - Optional "available for consultation" status.

- **Scholar Matching Engine**
  - User describes need ("my son is confused about music in Islam", "I want to learn how to give dawah to my non-Muslim colleagues").
  - System suggests 2-4 verified scholars with relevant expertise + location/language match.

- **Public + Private Q&A (Ask a Scholar)**
  - Public answered questions become a growing knowledge base (searchable, categorized, with scholar attribution).
  - Private consultations routed as leads.
  - Clear disclaimers + escalation to local mufti when needed.

---

## 2. Masjid & Madrasa as Central Hubs

- **Rich Masjid Profiles**
  - Prayer times (auto or manual)
  - Weekly programs, khutbah archive (text or audio)
  - Sisters' facilities, youth programs, library info
  - "This masjid recommends" verified vendors (electrician, cleaner, caterer for events)

- **Masjid Needs Board (Reverse Leads)**
  - Masjid posts needs publicly or to verified network:
    - "Need 3 volunteers for iftar distribution"
    - "Seeking qualified female hifz teacher"
    - "Urgent: plumber for wudu area leak"
  - Volunteers and providers respond directly.

- **Madrasa Discovery + Parent Tools**
  - Curriculum highlights, teacher qualifications, fees transparency, reviews from parents focused on adab + ilm.

---

## 3. Dawah Content & Knowledge Layer

- **Verified Content Hub**
  - Short videos, articles, audio nasheeds/lectures, infographics.
  - Only from L4 verified scholars or approved da'ees.
  - Tagging: topic, audience (youth, sisters, reverts, parents), length.
  - "Share for dawah" cards optimized for WhatsApp/Facebook.

- **Curated Learning Paths**
  - "How to pray with khushu"
  - "Basics every new Muslim needs in first 30 days"
  - "Raising children upon the Sunnah"
  - "Giving dawah with wisdom and beautiful manners"
  - Paths can be scholar-designed or AI-suggested from verified material (Phase 2+).

- **Community Dawah Campaigns**
  - Verified organizations launch campaigns.
  - Platform helps with volunteer matching, material distribution, impact tracking.
  - Example campaigns: "Return to the Masjid", "Protect Our Youth", "Ramadan Ready", "Family that Prays Together".

---

## 4. Special Populations & Reverts

- **Revert / New Muslim Welcome System**
  - Matching to a trained, verified "companion" or mentor.
  - Curated digital welcome pack ( Salah guide, basic aqeedah, dua book, local masjid intro).
  - Private support group option (with scholar oversight).
  - Milestone celebrations (first Ramadan, first fast, etc.) with consent.

- **Sisters-Specific & Youth-Specific Layers**
  - Gender-appropriate matching and content.
  - Youth mentors and role models (verified).
  - Issues relevant to modern fitnah (social media, dating culture, identity, mental health from Islamic lens).

---

## 5. Family & Societal Reform

- **Marriage & Family Islah**
  - Pre-marital counseling directory (scholar + counselor pairs).
  - Marriage resources based on Sunnah (rights, communication, conflict resolution).
  - Post-marriage support and referrals.

- **Community Moral Campaigns**
  - Coordinated efforts against widespread problems (interest-based loans, zina culture, neglect of salah, drug issues, family breakdown).
  - Run by coalitions of masjids + scholars + NGOs.
  - Platform provides coordination tools and visibility.

---

## 6. Charity, Waqf & Transparent Good Deeds

- **Impact-Focused Charity Discovery**
  - Verified campaigns with clear goals and regular updates.
  - "Where your sadaqah went" reports (not just receipts).
  - Waqf project visibility (building masjids, schools, water wells).

- **Skill-Based Volunteering**
  - "I am a graphic designer - I want to help dawah organizations 5 hours/week."
  - Matching to verified institutions needing help.

---

## 7. Leadership and Organization Support Angle (Advanced)

These make the coordination mission concrete:

- Aggregated, anonymized societal insight reports:
  - "Areas with highest demand for Islamic teachers"
  - "Common family challenges reported this quarter"
  - "Growth in revert inquiries in City X"

- Tools for large-scale coordination that a just leader would value (disaster response, nationwide education drives, standardization of certain community services).

- Optional "Societal Good Score" or impact dashboard for institutions (how many people they helped via the platform).

**Critical guardrail:** Never surveillance. Only opt-in, aggregated, beneficial data.

---

## 8. Technical & Experience Ideas

- Intent parser trained on deen + dunya queries ("I want to start praying tahajjud", "Need halal mortgage advice", "My friend wants to accept Islam").
- "One click for barakah" - quick actions like "Find nearest masjid with Maghrib program tonight".
- Integration points with masjid apps or existing Islamic tools (read-only where possible).
- Beautiful, calm, adab-respecting UI (avoid flashy ads in dawah sections).

---

## Phasing Recommendation

**P0 (MVP):** 
- Strong Islamic category taxonomy
- Basic scholar/teacher profiles with L4
- Masjid profiles + simple needs
- Quran/hifz discovery

**P1:**
- Content hub (articles + short audio/video)
- Ask Scholar basic routing
- Revert welcome starter
- Family counseling directory

**P2+:**
- Full campaigns engine
- Personalized growth paths
- Advanced scholar tools + public Q&A knowledge base
- Leadership insight views (aggregated)

---

## Success Metrics Specific to Dawah/Islah

- Number of verified scholars & da'ees active
- Number of people matched to Islamic education / scholar consults
- Revert / new Muslim support cases opened + positive feedback
- Masjid needs fulfilled through the platform
- Content views + shares from verified sources
- Qualitative stories of people coming closer to deen or solving family issues Islamically
- Institutional adoption (how many masjids actively use the needs board)

---

**Remember the mission:**

Taqdimah is not here to become the biggest "Islamic app."  
It is a sincere system placed in service of the Ummah so that society becomes more just, more knowledgeable, more connected in good, more economically resilient, and more pleasing to Allah.

May Allah accept this effort and make it a means of barakah and islah. Ameen.
